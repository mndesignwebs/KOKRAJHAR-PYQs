This website is exact archive of previous year question papers (PYQs) designed to help students studying under the ASSEB Board in Kokrajhar.
All question papers are organized by class and exam type to make access simple and effective.
This project was initiated and developed by Mwnpung Narzary, a Class 10 student of Auxilium School, Kokrajhar, with the aim of supporting juniors and fellow students in their exam preparation.

The collection will be updated gradually with more papers from different years and classes. If you find this resource helpful, feel free to share it with your friends and classmates.
